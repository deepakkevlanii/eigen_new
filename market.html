Precision in Aerospace Manufacturing Compliance and Performance

As every part of an aircraft has to be made with extreme accuracy and precision, precision plays a crucial role in the Aerospace Manufacturing process. The design phase is very important for precision as manufacturing is based on the design. Even a small flaw can result in serious safety issues like the failure of vital aircraft components, loss of control, or even disastrous accidents.

Little deviations in complying with the rules can have significant repercussions, including safety concerns, and product problems. At Eigen, we are aware of the precision, care and skill needed to provide precision manufactured goods for the aerospace sector. Providing precise metal fabrication solutions for a range of cabin-to-cargo applications, our team has strong experience in the field.
