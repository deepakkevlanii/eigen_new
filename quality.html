AS 9100 D

Applicability (Purpose)

Certification to AS 9100 D qualifies Eigen to provide services to Aerospace industry clients.

Benefits

Certification to AS 9100 D is an assurance to our customers that, the QMS of our company meets the stringent requirements of the Aerospace Standard and that they can expect the consistent quality of service from us.

ISO 9001:2015

Applicability (Purpose)

ISO 9001:2015 is one more feather in Eigen’s cap signifying its excellence in manufacturing stamping parts and press tools for aerospace application.

Benefits

Eigen is constantly working towards providing the excellent results to clients. The certificate signifies Eigen’s efficient quality management system in manufacturing stamping parts and press tools for aerospace application.

ISO 45001:2018

Applicability (Purpose)

Certification to ISO 45001:2018 enables Eigen to manage its Occupational Health & Safety Risks and improve its OH&S performance. An OH&S management system will assist Eigen to fulfil its legal requirements and other requirements.

Benefits

Certification to ISO 45001:2018 offers many benefits for Eigen and their employees, from increased productivity to lower costs and better overall safety.
